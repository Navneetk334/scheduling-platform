## Persona profiles

### 1. Individuals
Personal scheduling (coffee chats, interviews, calls). **Core needs:** booking
page, availability rules, calendar sync, reminders, reschedule/cancel, dark
mode. Frictionless single-user onboarding; free tier.

### 2. Freelancers
Client-facing solo professionals. **Adds:** multiple event types, **payments +
invoices**, custom booking questions, branded page, embeddable widget on their
portfolio site. Buffers to avoid burnout.

### 3. Agencies
Multi-client, multi-member teams. **Adds:** teams/roles, **round robin** &
**collective** meetings, white label + **custom domains**, analytics, coupons,
CRM + Slack integrations, per-client branding.

### 4. Sales teams
High-volume outbound/inbound scheduling. **Adds:** **round robin with
routing/qualification forms**, CRM sync (contacts + activities), reminders
(SMS/WhatsApp) to cut no-shows, analytics, webhooks/API, AI assistant, PWA/
mobile for reps on the go.

### 5. HR / Recruiting
Interviews and candidate coordination. **Adds:** **collective** (panel)
interviews, group sessions, recurring cycles, custom forms (screening),
audit logs, permissions, reminders, localization for global hiring.

### 6. Healthcare
Patient appointments. **Adds:** group booking (classes), recurring visits,
**SMS/WhatsApp reminders** (no-show reduction), payments/invoices, strict
**GDPR/security**, buffers/limits, localization, reliability.

### 7. Education
Office hours, tutoring, classes, admissions. **Adds:** group booking, recurring
schedules, collective sessions, localization/RTL, white label per institution,
accessibility, analytics on utilization.

### 8. Enterprises
Large orgs with governance needs. **Adds:** **SSO/OAuth**, RBAC + permissions,
**audit logs**, security logs, **SOC2/GDPR-ready** architecture, white label +
custom domains, API/GraphQL/webhooks, subscriptions (seats), analytics, SLAs.

### 9. Consultants
Paid advisory sessions. **Adds:** **payments + coupons + invoices**, recurring
retainers, custom intake forms, branded/white-label pages, reminders across
channels, AI email generator for follow-ups.

### 10. Developers
Building on the platform. **Adds:** **REST + GraphQL APIs**, **OAuth apps**,
**webhooks**, typed SDK, API keys, rate limiting, embeddable widgets, sandbox,
and thorough docs.
